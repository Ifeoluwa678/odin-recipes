FOOTBALL RECIPES

Football is a game played by 22 players on he pitch, 11 players in each team and with one person as goal keeper in each team.

The remaining 10 players are distributed into various positions on the pitch, some of the positions we have are: the center backs, the full backs which are the right back and the left back, we also have the mieldfielders which can also be categorised in 5 classes; the LMF, RMF, CMF, AMF, and the DM

We also have the attacking position, they are known for goal scoring. we can also classified them based on the formaton being use by the manager; the LMF, SS, CF, RMF

Note that not all this position cannot be played by a team in a single match, the positioning depends on the manager formation
